Manali


Manali is a beautiful hill station in Himachal Pradesh, famous for its snow-covered mountains, scenic valleys, adventure sports, and pleasant weather. It is one of the most popular holiday destinations in India.

  1. Solang Valley - Famous for adventure activities.
  2. Rohtang Pass - Known for breathtaking snow views.
  3. Hadimba Temple - An ancient and famous temple.
  4. Manu Temple - A popular religious site.
  5. Old Manali - Known for its cafes and natural beauty.
  • Siddu - A traditional Himachali dish.
  • Trout Fish - A famous local delicacy.
  • Babru - A popular Himachali snack.
